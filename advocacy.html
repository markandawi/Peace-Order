Number Coding Scheme

Number coding scheme is one of the ordinances here in Baguio City. This is strictly implemented to lessen the number of vehicles per day and to control the traffic here in Baguio City. This is being implemented by the time of 7am to 7pm from Monday to Friday. This can be suspended by the advisory of the mayor. If you will violate this ordinance, you are obliged to pay 500 pesos to get your license. The car is prohibited if the plate number ends with these: Monday: 1 or 2 Tuesday: 3 or 4 Wednesday: 5 or 6 Thursday: 7 or 8 Friday: 9 or 0
